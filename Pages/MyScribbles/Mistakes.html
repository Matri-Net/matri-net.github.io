Mistakes

Making a mistake feels terrible
To make a mistake is nothing short of unbearable
Whether it be a test or a task
Your face, you want to mask


Realising you have done one
You get a metric ton
Of pain and sorrow
You delay the task until tomorrow


A fresh new day arises
But your body paralyzes
When it sees the sin you have comitted
The factor you have omitted


Starting over is harder than starting up
Only if you feel it so
Doing it is easier than drinking tea from a cup
So, do it, rework and go!
