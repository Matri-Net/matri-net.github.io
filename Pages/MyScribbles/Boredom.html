Boredom

An endless day
An endless night
No longer can I stay
Without joy or fright


No task to finish
No work to do
Causes my mind to diminish
Into a worn out goo


The housefly sees all
With its compound eyes
It sees its fall
It sees its demise


I have no game to play
I have no sound to listen
Time is what I have to slay
This is my mission


At the end of the long road
There is a light
Flow with the river
I might


Reaching the end
I look back
The river, my friend
was only an hour long
