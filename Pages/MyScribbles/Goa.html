An Ode To Goa

A view of wonder
A place to wander
A shore of sand
And a little of land
A vast sea
A lasting dream
A place to be
Full of gleam
Creatures of water
In India's daughter
Worn out shacks
Capitalism at max
Rich history
Places full of mystery
