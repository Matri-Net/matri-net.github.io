An Ode To Eggs

Round and small
Purest of them all
Still as a doll
Containing a little ball
Calcious shell
Opened, gives a smell
A lot to tell
Living inside only to be shown hell
Life, you produce
All but a muse
Filled with hues
A lot to use
Animals depend on you
From old to new
To reproduce
Thank you
Eggs
