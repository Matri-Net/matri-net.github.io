Up To The Mark

I am not here
Cannot reach the tier
Where I have to be
I hope nobody can see
That I am the bottom of the bag
The path of the mine goes zig zag
From good to bad
This wee lad
Has gone below the mark
The difference between the great and me is stark
I am not up to the mark
I guess I just have to climb the hill


The hill is hard
Has no bard
There lie spikes and obstacles
I am caught in the tentacles
of work
In the caves of happiness, I lurk


The mark grows higher
I have no desire
To folllow it
Not doing this destorys me bit by bit
I am not among you, I belong to a group another
I'm sorry Mother
