Trouble

The tiny housefly causes trouble for everyone
But what can it do?
With hate or terror it is not one
It just wants some food


Trying to escape it flies
Its buzzing creating noise
It tells no lies
But eats the faeces of rats and mice


The housefly sees all
With its compound eyes
It sees its fall
It sees its demise


It sees the trouble it causes
It is helpless to stop it
It just wants to live
Just wants peace in a pit
